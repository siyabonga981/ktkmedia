Digital Signage

Digital Signage delivers high quality multimedia output with durable, high performance displays, which are designed and customised to capture the customer’s attention. With our extensive content management tools it is easy to reach customers, employees and partners on any screen anywhere at any time. Retail Digital Signage provides retailers with effective, customised and visually-appealing content that enhances the customer shopping experience whilst driving engagement and a consistent brand image.

Corporate Communications

KTK Media offers an innovative way for clients to communicate with staff by using targeted channels featuring video, dynamic imagery and live data. A single, easy-to-use platform that creates, schedules and distributes stunning visual clarity and professional displays. Employees can access this information on a variety of devices, from desktops to tablets to display screens when and where they need to.

E-Learning

E-Learning or Electronic Learning is training, learning, or education delivered online through a computer or any other digital device. This makes it easy for users to learn anytime, anywhere, with few, if any, restrictions.

Room Booking Solutions – Scheduling Software

Room booking systems allow staff to book meeting rooms and equipment in the workplace using their email calendar or touch screens located outside the boardroom, streamlining workflow and promoting cooperation in the office. This simple boardroom solution allows staff to select and allocate booking times, down to the minute, avoiding miscommunication, conflict over office space, and unnecessary interruptions. With just a cursory glance at the touch screen or hallway signage located outside the boardroom, staff can see when a meeting room is available.
